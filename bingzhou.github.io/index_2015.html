Brief Biography

I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the North Carolina State University. I received my Master degree in 2014 and Bachelor degree in 2011 from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Jiangsu, China.

Research

My research interests include performance diagnosis, debugging and distributed systems. My advisor is Prof. Xiaohui (Helen) Gu. I am a member of DANCE (Distributed system research on Autonomy, resilieNce, Collaboration, and Energy).
